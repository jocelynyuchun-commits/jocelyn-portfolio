mission

Provide the Figma community with a documented, researched, and usable design systems example.

This file is part of an educational series from the Product Education team at Figma that covers all-things design systems, and is meant for those looking to get started or asking themselves if they need to get started.

This system demonstrates one way of constructing and managing a design system beyond just a UI kit.

This is not the only approach to design systems, but we hope it gives you confidence to tackle this problem for yourself and/or your organization.
